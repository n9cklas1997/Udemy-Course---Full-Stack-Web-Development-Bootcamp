Christopher Nolan Movies

My top 3 movies by Christopher Nolan

Memento

Memento is a psychological thriller that follows Leonard Shelby, a man suffering from short-term memory loss who is determined to find his wife's killer. The film is known for its unique non-linear narrative, which unfolds in reverse chronological order. Through its innovative storytelling, Memento explores themes of memory, identity, and self-deception.

Shutter Island

Shutter Island is a psychological mystery thriller starring Leonardo DiCaprio. The story follows U.S. Marshal Teddy Daniels as he investigates the disappearance of a patient from a remote psychiatric hospital on Shutter Island. As the investigation progresses, reality becomes increasingly uncertain, leading to a shocking and thought-provoking conclusion.

Inception

Inception is a science-fiction thriller that explores the concept of entering and manipulating dreams. The film follows Dom Cobb, a skilled thief who steals secrets from people's subconscious minds, as he undertakes a seemingly impossible mission known as "inception." Combining stunning visuals, complex storytelling, and philosophical themes, Inception challenges viewers to question the nature of reality and perception.
